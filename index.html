UGV Tracking with UAV

A UAV has to track a UGV. As a UGV a turtle bot has been used and as UAV Parrot AR Drone 2.0 has been used. The ground robot will be tracking a predifined trajectory. The UAV has to track the UGV exactly on the top of it. The main attractive of this project is, as a controller a reinforcement learning method has been implemented.
Reinforcement Learning Controller
Instead of I telling the drone to track the UGV, I have designed a learning method which will learn to track the UGV. The method has been trained for 10000 trails. After learning, the UAV perfectly tracked the UGV exactly on the top of it. (very small offset had been observed at some points)
